## Tableau Relationship Map

| Fact Column | Operator | Dimension Column | Dimension Table |
|---|---|---|---|
| `DATE_KEY` | `=` | `DATE_KEY (DIM_DATE)` | `DIM_DATE` |
| `SUPPORT_CATEGORY_KEY` | `=` | `SUPPORT_CATEGORY_KEY (DIM_SUPPORT_CATEGORY)` | `DIM_SUPPORT_CATEGORY` |
| `USER_KEY` | `=` | `USER_KEY (DIM_USER)` | `DIM_USER` |

> **Bridge column rule:** Every op string from the relationship expressions above must exist
> as a **physical column** in **every sheet**. This includes both the plain FK side
> (`DATE_KEY`, `SUPPORT_CATEGORY_KEY`, `USER_KEY`) and the qualified PK side
> (`DATE_KEY (DIM_DATE)`, `SUPPORT_CATEGORY_KEY (DIM_SUPPORT_CATEGORY)`, `USER_KEY (DIM_USER)`).

## Bridge Column Summary (All Sheets)

| Bridge Column | Type | Present In |
|---|---|---|
| `DATE_KEY` | `DATE` | All 4 sheets |
| `DATE_KEY (DIM_DATE)` | `DATE` | All 4 sheets |
| `SUPPORT_CATEGORY_KEY` | `VARCHAR(255)` | All 4 sheets |
| `SUPPORT_CATEGORY_KEY (DIM_SUPPORT_CATEGORY)` | `VARCHAR(255)` | All 4 sheets |
| `USER_KEY` | `VARCHAR(255)` | All 4 sheets |
| `USER_KEY (DIM_USER)` | `VARCHAR(255)` | All 4 sheets |

> **Why quoted column names?** Column names containing spaces and parentheses
> (e.g. `DATE_KEY (DIM_DATE)`) must be wrapped in double quotes in standard SQL.
> In Excel these appear as regular column headers with spaces and parentheses as-is.
